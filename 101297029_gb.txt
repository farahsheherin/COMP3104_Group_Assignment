George Brown college is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario. The college was established in 1967. It was named
after a renowned 19th century politician and newspaper publisher, George Brown. The college has three campuses
in downtown Toronto – Casa Loma campus, St. James campus and Waterfront campus. It offers a wide range of programs in
different area of study such as art and design, business, construction and engineering technologies, hospitality and
culinary arts, early childhood education etc. The college offers 35 diploma programs, 31 advanced diploma programs and
8 degree programs (“George Brown College,” n.d.).
